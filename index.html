What We Do

E-Cell organises a variety of events throughout the year to promote entrepreneurial activities in and around the campus. Our annual E-Summit witnesses huge participation from all over India. Events like Ink-Fluence and Mega-Minds, Idea-Expo, Innovation@99 take the learning to a whole new level. E-Cell also conducts various lectures and workshops focussed at budding entrepreneurs. StartUp Internship Programme helps UEMJ students land internships in start-ups and get to know their working in greater depth.

Virtual Reality Startup Simulations

The Entrepreneurship Cell (E-Cell) in Jaipur could introduce Virtual Reality (VR) startup simulations to provide immersive, hands-on experience for aspiring entrepreneurs. By creating a VR platform where users can simulate running a business, managing resources, and tackling real-world challenges in a virtual environment, E-Cell can offer an engaging, interactive learning experience. This approach allows users to experiment with business strategies, make decisions, and witness outcomes in a risk-free setting, bridging the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application. It enhances learning by providing realistic scenarios, fostering innovation, and better preparing entrepreneurs for the complexities of starting and managing a business.
